HOW TO PLAY

📖
Read the Story

Each scene presents a narrative. Wait for the text to finish typing or click to skip.

🔘
Make Choices

Select from available choices. Each decision shapes your fate and changes the story.

💾
Progress Saved

Your progress is automatically saved. Resume anytime from the main menu.

🎧
Use Headphones

Audio is a core part of the experience. Headphones strongly recommended.

⏸
Pause Anytime

Press ESC or the pause button during gameplay to open the pause menu.
